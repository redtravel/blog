    About "seo friendly templates":
    
    1. seo firendly page title
    
    The default title format is <title> blog name | post title </title> 
    For Search Engine Optimization (SEO) reason, the title of the page should be in the following format : 
    post title | category name (optionally) | blog name(optionally)
    example:
    url:
    http://lifetype.chinese-blog.org/lifetype-blog/lifetype-seo-firendly-page-title
    title:
    <title> seo friendly templates | lifetype templates | lifetype¡¡</title>
    
    2 html tag of "post name"
    
    category page:
    <h1>category name</h1>
    <h2>post name</h2>
    
    post page:
    <h1>post name</h1>
    
    3   Force search engine To Read Your post content First
    
    Search Engine views your webpage from top left, to bottom right. But because most websites contain a left hand column which contains all of their navigation links...
    
    You need not to know more about Search Engine friendly design, just use this template.
    
    
    4 The Page Is Valid XHTML 1.0 Strict! 
    
    5 And more...
    
    Remove Calendar in sidebar(not very useful), Recent articles shoud be on the top of sidebar(what search engine and reader interest in, move author and publish date to the bottom of post page...
